## What is IPV4?

- IPv4 addresses are 32 bits long.

` 10 → 00001010
  0 → 00000000
 15 → 00001111
 25 → 00011001`

`IPv4 Address (Decimal): 10.0.15.25
IPv4 Address (Binary):  00001010.00000000.00001111.00011001`

- An IPv4 address has two parts the network portion and the host portion.

- IPv4 has five address classes each with its own range of IP addresses.


## IPv4 Address Classes

| **Class** | **IP Address Range** | **Default Subnet Mask** | **Purpose** |
|:---------:|:---------------------|:-----------------------:|-------------|
| A | 1.0.0.0 – 126.255.255.255 | 255.0.0.0 (/8) | Large networks |
| B | 128.0.0.0 – 191.255.255.255 | 255.255.0.0 (/16) | Medium-sized networks |
| C | 192.0.0.0 – 223.255.255.255 | 255.255.255.0 (/24) | Small networks |
| D | 224.0.0.0 – 239.255.255.255 | N/A | Multicast |
| E | 240.0.0.0 – 255.255.255.255 | N/A | Experimental |
